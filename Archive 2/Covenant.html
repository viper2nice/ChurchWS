lighthouse baptist church covenant

Having been led, as we believe, by the Spirit of God, to receive the Lord Jesus Christ as our personal Savior and Lord, and on the profession of our faith, and having been baptized in the name of The Father, The Son, and The Holy Ghost, we do solemnly and joyfully covenant with each other to walk together in him with brotherly love, to His glory. Corinthians 6:19-20.

WE THEREFORE IN HIS STRENGTH PLEDGE

 That we will exercise a Christian care and watchfulness over each other and faithfully warn, exhort and admonish each other as occasion may require.

 That we will not forsake the assembling of ourselves together, but will uphold the public worship of our God and will by His grace attend all meetings of the church and observe the ordinances. Hebrews 10:24-25

 That we will respect the bonds of marriage, strive to uphold the unity of the home and pray privately with our family, endeavoring to fulfill the great duty of bringing up children and those under our care in the fear of the Lord for the Salvation of their souls, the service of the Lord and the enjoyment of heaven. Matthew 19:1-9; Hebrews. 13:4; Ephesians. 5:24-25: Ephesians 6:4

 That we accept the Bible, the Word of God, as our only creed for faith and practice. That we will study it prayerfully, obey it diligently and share it willingly. II Timothy 2:15; II Timothy 3:16.

 That as the Light of the World and the salt of the earth, relying on the Holy Spirit who dwells within us, we will deny ungodliness and every worldly lust, and walking circumspectly in the world, that we may glorify God and win the souls of men. Matthew 5:13, 16; Titus 2:11-12.

 That we will abstain from carnal indulgencies, adultery, fornication, drunkenness, disorderly conduct, smoking, lodges or any secret order or witchcraft. That we will separate ourselves unto God and live soberly, righteously, and Godly in this present world. II Cor. 6:14; 1 John 2:16-17; 1 Cor. 6:18-20: Gal. 5:20

 That we will abstain from intoxicating drinks as beverage and abstain from the sale, the use, and the growth of tobacco. 1Thess. 5:22; Ephesians. 5:18

 That we keep ourselves from gambling, raffles, lotteries, and all questionable games and betting. 1 Thess. 5:22, Ephesians 5:18

 That we keep ourselves from worldly pleasures, worldly dressings and will not support or be a part of any organization that promotes worldliness or immodesty. 1 John 2:15; Hebrews 1:24-26; 1 Peter 3:1-4.

 That we will contribute cheerfully and regularly of our tithes and offerings as the Lord provides to the church for the support of the ministry, the expenses of the church, the relief of the poor and the spread of the gospel throughout the world. Matthew 28:18-20; II Corinthians 9:6-8; Malachi 3:8-10

 That we will be faithful in our jobs and give an honest days’ work for an honest days’ pay to those we serve, as well as an honest days’ pay for an honest days’ work to those who serve us. Ephesians 6:5-9

 That we will in all conditions, even until death, strive to live to the glory of our Lord and Master and to be a faithful witness of Him who hath called us out of darkness into His marvelous light. 1 Peter 2:9
